## BLOCKQUOTES RULES

For blockquoting Markdown uses email-style ">" characters before every line.

Nested blockquotes are allowed. The level of blockquote nesting is determined by the number of characters ">" in line.

___

## EXAMPLES:
># **Sample bold Header**
>
>First paragraph
>
>Second paragraph
>
>>Nested blockquote 
>>
>>>Nested blockquote 
>
> *Other cursive text...*
>
>***
>
> Horizontal rule
